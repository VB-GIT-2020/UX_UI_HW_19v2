EMV Bank
Mobile Banking App

AN APP MADE FOR MILLENNIAL CUSTOMERS
The EMV mobile banking app encourages and
supports Millennials by helping with financial
management and education to encourage
saving for financial security and peace of mind.
It was no surprise that we found Millennials were
excited about having all their financial needs met on
a single platform! What did come as a surprise was
that they were more intrigued at the thought of
their financial education needs being by a bank,
and that banks could care for their financial future!

We are grateful that they shared their
vulnerability with us researchers.
SAFTRIP Mobile
Travel App

AN APP THAT MAKES INTERNATIONAL
TRAVELLERS FEEL CONNECTED
The SafTrip app empowers international
travellers by connecting with fellow travellers
during stopovers at airports. I thought international travellers would use
the app to be alerted of any health and safety issues from fellow travelers.

What I did not expect was the emaotional need for
community support that was immediately
apparent from lonely travelers in these
uncertain COVID times, due to which the chat
feature was instantly accepted.
